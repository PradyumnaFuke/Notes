#1

If the power of any non-zero number is \(0\) then the result will be \(1\). \[\colorbox{ #f2f2f2 }{\(\color{ #2f2f2f }x^0=1\)}\] \[\text{e.g., } \begin{aligned} 8^0&=1 \\ -4^0&=1 \\ e^0&=1^* \\ \end{aligned} \] \({}^*e=2.718\)

#2

Negative property of exponent where \(x\) is a non-zero number: \[\colorbox{ #f2f2f2 }{\(\color{ #3f3f3f }x^n=\frac{1}{x^{-n}}\)}\] \[\colorbox{ #f2f2f2 }{\(\color{ #2f2f2f }x^{-n}=\frac{1}{x^{n}}\)}\] \[\text{e.g., } \begin{aligned} 2^4&=\frac{1}{2^{-4}} \\ 10^{-4}&=\frac{1}{10^{4}} \\ \end{aligned} \]
